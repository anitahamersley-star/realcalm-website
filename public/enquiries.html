Our process

Enquiry and intake in four steps

Transparent and paced to suit you. We will keep you informed at every stage.

1. Submit enquiry

Complete the intake form so we understand your needs, preferences and funding pathway.

2. Triage and match

We review your information, consider fit for services, and provide an estimated start time.

3. Confirm and book

If a booking is suitable, we confirm details, fees and paperwork, then schedule your first appointment.

4. First session

We begin with a warm initial session to hear your goals, answer questions and plan next steps.
