Therapy

Therapy services

Practical, reflective support shaped around what matters to you.

Individual therapy

Identity-affirming sessions for adults and adolescents.

  • Support for anxiety, burnout, mood, adjustment and life transitions
  • Strengths-based strategies for autistic and ADHD clients
  • Telehealth and in-person appointments available

Child and adolescent therapy

Gentle, engaging sessions that respect each young person's pace.

  • Emotion regulation, sensory needs and self-advocacy
  • Building communication and social connection without masking

Parent and caregiver coaching

Practical guidance to create calmer family rhythms.

  • Understanding behaviour through a nervous system lens
  • Co-regulation and connection-focused strategies
  • Planning supports across home, school and community
Assessments

Assessment services

Clear, compassionate assessments that translate results into meaningful recommendations.

Autism and ADHD assessments

Neuroaffirming processes for children, adolescents and adults.

Assessments that clarify how you learn and process information.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Key details about how we deliver therapy and assessments.

Do you offer telehealth?
Yes. We provide secure video sessions for clients across Australia, as well as in-person appointments in Albany.
Do I need a referral?
No referral is required to book. If you plan to use Medicare rebates, please bring a valid referral and Mental Health Treatment Plan.
Can I use my NDIS funding?
Yes. We see self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants. We can also liaise with your support coordinator if helpful.
How long are sessions?
Standard therapy sessions are 50 minutes. Assessment sessions and feedback appointments vary depending on the assessment plan.
What happens in the first session?
We discuss your goals, history, preferences and any immediate concerns. Together we agree on next steps and a pace that feels right for you.
What if I need to reschedule?
Please let us know as soon as possible. Our cancellation policy will be discussed when booking so expectations are clear.
