Policy

Cancellation and rescheduling

We keep a limited number of appointments each week to ensure thoughtful care. Clear notice helps us offer times to others on the waitlist.

Key points

  • Please give at least 48 hours notice to reschedule or cancel.
  • Late cancellations (less than 48 hours) may be charged up to 100% of the fee.
  • NDIS cancellations follow the current NDIS cancellation rules.
  • If you are unwell or facing an emergency, let us know as soon as possible.
  • We can arrange telehealth if you cannot attend in person.

If you have questions about the policy or accessibility needs, please contact us so we can plan together.
