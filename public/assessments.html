Our process

Four steps to clarity

Supportive, paced steps so you know what will happen and when.

1. Pre-assessment session

    The psychologist will meet with you to discuss the process, gather background information and answer any questions.

    This may include exploring developmental history, current challenges and strengths.

    Following this session, you will be provided with an obligation-free quotation for the full assessment.

2. Assessment

    The psychologist will administer assessment tools with you over one or more sessions, depending on your needs and goals.

    These may include clinical interview, cognitive testing or observational measures.

    You will be asked to complete standardised questionnaires and to nominate someone who knows you well to provide additional information.

3. Interpretation

    The psychologist will explore the diagnostic criteria and review the information gathered during the assessment.

    They will consider what the results mean for you, including strengths, differences and support needs.

    This process may take several weeks depending on the complexity of the assessment.

4. Report and feedback session

    You will be provided with a comprehensive report summarising the assessment findings.

    The psychologist will meet with you to discuss the results, answer questions and provide guidance on next steps.

    This may include recommendations for supports, accommodations or referrals to other services

Additional information

Assessments are paced to reduce overwhelm and focus on what will help you most.

Therapy

    Therapy is available following assessment to help individuals and their families navigate any diagnosis in a way that promotes wellbeing, self-acceptance and personal growth.

Fees

    Fees vary depending on the specific requirements of each individual and a quotation is provided following the pre-assessment session.

Referral

    You do not require a referral for an assessment, however it is recommended that you discuss this with your GP.
